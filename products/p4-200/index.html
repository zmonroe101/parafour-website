The Highest-Capacity P4 Series Dispenser

The P4-200 is Parafour's dual-hose flagship — designed for high-throughput autogas stations, large fleet operations, and retail forecourt installations that require maximum capacity and full public-access retail capability.

Designed for the Gasguard GG20 nozzle, the P4-200 is fully NTEP certified for installation with NTEP-approved meters and fuel management systems. It carries the same ETL listing, CE marking, and GOST certification as all P4 Series models — built and tested to UL 495, UL 1238, NFPA 58, and NFPA 30A.

Standard Features

Every P4-200 ships with the complete P4 Series feature set on both hose positions — standard, no packages required.

🖥

Electronic Display

Fully NCWM retail-approved electronic display — legal for all commercial autogas transactions, public-access retail, and fleet fueling.

▶

Push-To-Start / Stop

Illuminated push-button start/stop with ON indicator on both hose positions for clear, independent operation at each dispensing point.

⚙

Electronic Calibration

Field calibration via the electronic menu system. No mechanical seals required. All calibration events logged in the NTEP Category 1 event log.

🌡

Automatic Temperature Compensation

Standard ATC per API 2450 / ASTM D 1250 / ISO R91 on both hose positions for temperature-corrected, legally traceable volume transactions.

💲

Multi-Level Pricing

Dual product pricing or up to 5-level pricing — configure retail, fleet, and wholesale rate structures independently on each hose without external controllers.

📊

Electronic Totalizer

Per-hose and cumulative totalizers in non-volatile memory. Full shift reporting, sales history, and transaction logs via the onboard menu system.

💳

Card Reader Pre-Wired

Factory pre-wired for most commercial card readers and fuel management systems on both hose positions — ready for integration without field modification.

🔒

Solenoid Flow Control

Independent electronic solenoid valves on each hose for precise flow control, accurate metering, and positive anti-siphon shutoff on both dispensing positions.
