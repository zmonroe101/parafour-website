P4-150

Standard Features

The P4-150 includes the full P4 Series feature set in a dual-hose configuration.

🖥

Electronic Display

Fully NCWM retail-approved electronic display — legal for cylinder fill and vehicle fueling transactions in commercial settings.

▶

Push-To-Start / Stop

Illuminated push-button start/stop on both hose positions with clear ON indication for each dispensing point.

⚙

Electronic Calibration

Field-configurable via the electronic menu. No mechanical seals required. Calibration events logged in the NTEP Category 1 event log.

🌡

Automatic Temperature Compensation

Standard ATC per API 2450 / ASTM D 1250 ensures volume-corrected, legally traceable transactions from both dispensing positions.

💲

Multi-Level Pricing

Dual product pricing or up to 5-level pricing — configure different rates for cylinder fill versus vehicle fill from the same unit.

📊

Electronic Totalizer

Per-hose and cumulative totalizers maintained in non-volatile memory with shift reporting and sales history.

💳

Card Reader Pre-Wired

Factory pre-wired for most commercial card readers and fuel management systems for both dispensing positions.

🔒

Solenoid Flow Control

Electronic solenoid valve on each hose position for independent flow control, accurate metering, and anti-siphon security.

Ideal Applications

Autogas + Cylinder Fill

Fueling sites that serve both autogas vehicles and cylinder exchange or fill customers from a single dispenser location.

RV Parks & Campgrounds

Dual-hose setup allows simultaneous or sequential RV tank and portable cylinder filling with a single certified dispenser.

Propane Retail Locations

Propane dealers who offer both bulk-fill and retail cylinder services can consolidate into one NTEP-certified dispensing point.
