Modernize Your Existing Dispenser Cabinet

The P4-050 is a standalone electronic register designed to retrofit existing mechanical LPG dispensing cabinets. Rather than replacing an entire dispenser, the P4-050 adds full electronic metering, display, and NTEP certification to infrastructure you already own.

Compatible with most NTEP-certified PD and mass flow meters, the P4-050 is certified as a Retail Motor Fuel Dispenser (RMFD) under NIST Handbook 44. It includes no-flow timeout and zero setback functions required for full Handbook 44 compliance.

Key Features

Full electronic motorfuel dispenser functionality in a retrofit register package.

📋

NTEP RMFD Certified

Certified as a Retail Motor Fuel Dispenser under NIST Handbook 44 — legal for commercial retail and fleet LPG transactions.

⚙

Broad Meter Compatibility

Compatible with most NTEP-certified positive displacement (PD) and mass flow meters — works with your existing metering hardware.

🛡

No-Flow Timeout & Zero Setback

Handbook 44 required safety functions built in — no-flow timeout halts transactions automatically; zero setback prevents false readings at transaction start.

🖥

Electronic Display

Clear electronic display for volume and dollar totals — replaces mechanical register dials with a legally traceable digital readout.

💲

Configurable Pricing

Supports multiple price levels — configure retail, fleet, or wholesale rate structures directly from the register.

🔧

Retrofit Design

Designed to install into existing mechanical dispensing cabinets — no new dispenser enclosure required. Reduces upgrade cost significantly.
