Modernize Your Existing LPG Cabinet

The P4-050 is a drop-in electronic register retrofit for existing mechanical LPG dispensing cabinets. Rather than replacing a functioning dispenser enclosure and piping, the P4-050 replaces only the mechanical register with a fully electronic, NTEP-certified measuring system.

Certified as a Retail Motor Fuel Dispenser (RMFD) under NTEP and compliant with NIST Handbook 44, the P4-050 brings legal-for-trade electronic dispensing to installations that previously operated with mechanical registers — without the cost of a full dispenser replacement.

Standard Features

Every P4-050 ships with the full Parafour electronic register feature set.

🖥

Electronic Display

NCWM retail-approved electronic display for volume and dollar totals — legal-for-trade in all commercial LPG dispensing applications.

⚙

Electronic Calibration

Field calibration via the electronic menu — no mechanical seals required. All calibration events logged in the NTEP RMFD event log.

🌡

Automatic Temperature Compensation

Standard ATC adjusts delivered volume to the 60°F reference standard per API 2450 / ASTM D 1250 for legally traceable transactions.

💲

Multi-Level Pricing

Dual product pricing or up to 5-level pricing for fleet rate tiers, customer classes, or shift-based pricing strategies.

📊

Electronic Totalizer

Cumulative volume and transaction totalizer maintained in non-volatile memory — accessible at any time from the menu system.

💳

Card Reader Pre-Wired

Factory pre-wired for most commercial card reader and fuel management systems — no field wiring modifications needed.

🔒

Solenoid Flow Control

Electronic solenoid valve controls product flow for metering accuracy and provides a positive anti-siphon shutoff.

▶

Push-To-Start / Stop

Illuminated push-button start/stop with ON indicator for clear, intuitive dispenser operation.

Compatibility & Installation

Compatible Meters

  • Positive displacement (PD) meters
  • Mass flow meters
  • Liqua-Tech LPM102
  • Neptune 4DMD
  • Liquid Controls MA4

Installation Notes

  • Retrofits into existing mechanical cabinet openings
  • Retains existing piping and enclosure
  • Remote pump mounting compatible
  • Third-party fuel management pedestal compatible

Optional Equipment

  • 2″ receipt printer
  • Integrated motor-starter with overload protection
  • Upgrade to P4-ParaFUEL fuel management system
