Standard Features

Every P4-100 ships with these features standard — no add-on packages required.

🖥

Electronic Display

Fully NCWM retail-approved electronic display for volume and dollar totals — legal for commercial fuel transactions in all U.S. jurisdictions.

▶

Push-To-Start / Stop

Illuminated push-button start/stop with ON indicator for clear, intuitive dispenser operation in fleet and public-access environments.

⚙

Electronic Calibration

Field calibration via the electronic menu — no mechanical seals required. All calibration events are logged in the NTEP Category 1 electronic event log.

🌡

Automatic Temperature Compensation

Standard ATC adjusts delivered volume to the 60°F reference standard per API 2450 / ASTM D 1250 / ISO R91 for legally traceable, temperature-corrected transactions.

💲

Multi-Level Pricing

Dual product pricing or up to 5-level pricing supports fleet rate tiers, customer classes, retail/wholesale splits, or shift-based pricing without external controllers.

📊

Electronic Totalizer

Cumulative volume and transaction totals maintained in non-volatile memory. Shift reporting and sales history accessible through the onboard menu system.

💳

Card Reader Pre-Wired

Factory pre-wired for most commercial card readers and fuel management systems. Integrates with third-party pedestals and authorization networks without field modifications.

🔒

Solenoid Flow Control

Electronic solenoid valve provides precise flow control for metering accuracy and a positive anti-siphon shutoff — protecting against product theft and unauthorized dispensing.
