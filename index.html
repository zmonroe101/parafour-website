Why Parafour Innovations

We don't just meet standards — we helped write them.

ETL-Listed Manufacturing

Every P4 Series dispenser carries the ETL mark, independently certified to ANSI/UL standards for LPG dispensing equipment used in commercial and fleet applications.

⚖

NTEP Category 1 Certified

Our dispensers hold NTEP Certificate of Conformance — the highest weights-and-measures certification for commercial fuel sales, legal for trade across all 50 states.

NFPA & NEC Compliant
