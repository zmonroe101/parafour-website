About Parafour Innovations

Dedicated to innovation, technology, quality, and flexibility in LPG dispensing systems.

Who We Are

Parafour Innovations, LLC is a company dedicated to innovation, technology, quality, and flexibility in LPG dispensing systems. While a newer company, Parafour brings more than 50 years of combined experience in the LPG fueling and Autogas industry. With experience gained working for companies like Petrolane, IMPCO, BRC, CleanFUEL USA, and GE, our management and engineering team has designed, built, and serviced thousands of Autogas refueling stations worldwide.

Our Team

Robin Parsons

President & Founder

Robin founded Parafour Innovations in January 2012, bringing decades of hands-on experience in LPG fueling systems design, manufacturing, and standards development to build the company from the ground up.

Jake Sutton

Production Manager

Jake joined Parafour in June 2015. He holds a B.B.A. in Management from Texas State University and oversees manufacturing operations and quality control for the P4 Series dispenser line.
